Opt Out of WarnerMedia Interest Based Advertising

As described in our privacy policy, WarnerMedia may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to collect information across our own or others’ websites, services, and apps over time. We may use and transfer this information to help serve advertising that may be more relevant to your interests on and off our Sites and across your devices and browsers. This type of advertising is known as interest-based advertising.

Opt Out of Interest-Based Advertising by WarnerMedia

If you opt out here, we will continue to collect data for non-interest-based advertising purposes, including research, analytics, and internal operations. You may continue to receive advertising on WarnerMedia websites and apps based on your activities across our properties.

Your opt-out choice will apply to the browser or device where you make the choice. This means that WarnerMedia will not deliver interest-based advertising to this browser or device and we will not otherwise use or transfer data collected on this browser or device for interest-based advertising.
